How-To Guides

Keyboard Shortcuts
  • Ctrl + Shift + O - Open reports panel
  • Ctrl + Shift + R - Generate rating URL
  • Ctrl + Shift + U - Unassign & Close
  • Ctrl + Shift + S - Submit report
  • Ctrl + Shift + X - Submit report and close panel
Using Reports Automator
  1. 1. Make sure you set your name and # of seconds in the fields above. Make sure your name matches the one listed in the post-interaction report form
  2. 2. After saving your address and email on this page, it will be saved, but won't visually show up in the input fields again
  3. 2. Being in the intercom tab, click the panda icon, then "Open/Close Reports Panel" (or use the shortcut). This will open the panel for your active conversation. Try it out!
  4. 3. If you select another conversation while the panel is open, it'll update with that conversation's info
  5. 4. Fill the required fields and submit. Real report form will automatically be filled, submitted and closed. You'll get a notification whether the report suceeded or failed.
  6. 5. If you've already submitted a report for a conversation, you'll see a notification about this in the panel (to avoid double entries).
  7. 6. To close the panel, click the red X button, or "Open/Close Reports Panel" again. Unlike before, panel now stops showing on every click (so it's not annoying anymore)
